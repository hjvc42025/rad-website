What’s rad?

Reel Action Digital is built on a single insight , every business has a content genre that grows its audience organically. We find it.

Our approach

We lead with organic.

rad is one of the few social media specialists with a genuine focus on organic content: content that earns its views without paid advertising or boosting.

Why does that matter? Because social media algorithms actively limit content that doesn’t earn engagement on its own. If your content isn’t performing organically, the platform simply won’t show it. And if you’re already paying for views on top of that, you’re paying more for less, because paid content is still scored against the same organic signals. Ask any Meta or TikTok specialist who’ll be straight with you.

People are also increasingly turning to social media to discover new businesses, services and people to hire. If they land on a dead account, or you’re not there at all. That’s revenue walking to someone who is.

We don’t just keep your account active. We build content that gives your business a distinct identity, turns followers into loyal customers, and creates advocates who come back, and bring others with them.

The rad story

From photography studio
to social media specialist

Reel Action Digital grew out of HJ Media, a business Hugo built on five-plus years of commercial photography. Good eye, good gear, and a real understanding of what makes a visual land. But one thing became clear fast: on social media, short-form video is what actually moves people. Not stills. Not long-form. Short, watchable video that earns the stop.

Hugo's particular strength is getting people talking to camera naturally. Not stiff or over-rehearsed. Real conversations that let your audience feel like they genuinely know the person behind the business. That familiarity is what builds trust, and trust is what converts a follower into a customer. It's the difference between a business people scroll past and one they actually reach out to.

After two years producing short-form content every day for clients across professional services, trades, finance and community work, we've built a recipe for content people actually stop and watch. We've also worked out how to make you feel relaxed and look natural in front of a lens, which for most people is the hard part.

Hugo still shoots photography on the side, with regular contributions to NZV8 and NZ Performance Car magazine alongside other commercial work. But the main game is short-form content, and helping businesses build an audience that actually grows.

How much does it cost? +

We have four services. Social media (Everything), where we handle the shoot, is from $1,899 +GST per month. Social media (You Shoot), where you film and we take care of the rest, is from $959 +GST per month. The Reels one-off shoot is $799 +GST with no ongoing commitment. Photography (half or full day) starts from $399 +GST. Every client gets a tailored quote after the initial call.

What platforms do you manage? +

Both retainer packages cover Instagram, Facebook and TikTok, with up to four posts per week. We don't currently offer LinkedIn management. The LinkedIn algorithm operates very differently to other platforms and requires a distinct content strategy to be effective. Including it as a bolt-on to these packages wouldn't do it justice.

How long until we see results? +

There's usually a teething period early on while we fine-tune your specific strategy and brand. During this time you may see outliers in both directions, some content performing unexpectedly well and some underperforming. That's normal and expected. By the two to three month mark we'll typically have enough data to know exactly what's working for your audience and have things well dialled in from there.

Do you work outside Tauranga? +

Yes. Social media management is done remotely, so rad works with businesses across New Zealand and internationally. For photo and video shoots we're based in Tauranga and travel within the Bay of Plenty and beyond.

What is the minimum commitment? +

Both retainer packages have a four-month minimum. This isn't arbitrary. The first months are where we dial in your content strategy, learn your brand, and edit the first batches of content to a standard we're confident in. Cutting that short doesn't give the process a fair chance. If you need to exit during the minimum period, early break fees and a non-disclosure agreement will apply. After the minimum, you can move to month-by-month, quarterly, bi-annual or annual. The Reels one-off shoot has no ongoing commitment.

What’s in the performance report? +

Each month we send a performance report built around rad’s own reporting framework. Unlike the dashboards you’d get from a paid ads specialist, which are built around spend metrics like cost-per-click and return on ad spend, our report focuses on the metrics that tell you whether your content is actually working organically. That includes reach and impressions (how many people your content is getting in front of), engagement rate (the percentage of viewers who interact with a post), video completion rate and average watch time (which tell us how well your content is holding attention), follower growth over the period, profile visits and link-in-bio clicks, and a breakdown of which content formats and topics are outperforming others. The goal is to tell you what’s working and why, so we can build more of what your audience responds to and cut what it doesn’t. Most paid ads reports tell you what your money is doing. Ours tells you what your content is doing.

Do we approve content before it goes live? +

Before we start posting, we'll send through an example batch so you can see the style and format we're working with. Once a format is approved, we post on the basis that you're happy with that style going forward. If you'd prefer to approve every individual piece of content before it goes live, we can arrange a system for that. Just let us know upfront and we'll build it into the workflow.

Do you run paid advertising or boost posts? +

Our focus is organic content. We advise waiting until there's a solid dataset of content to sample and judge performance from before moving into a paid ads campaign. Paid spend on content that hasn't proven itself organically tends to underperform and cost more. If you already work with a Meta or TikTok ads specialist, we'd love to collaborate. We can create a run of videos fine-tuned specifically for paid application to feed into their campaigns.

What if we are not sure social media is right for our business? +

That's what the free call is for. We'll look at your business and tell you whether organic content marketing is the right move, and if it is, what formula we'd build around. If it's not the right fit, we'll say so.
